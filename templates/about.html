About Energy Dashboard

Smart Campus Energy Management System

Overview

The Energy Consumption Dashboard is an advanced IoT-powered platform designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize energy usage across campus buildings in real-time. Using cutting-edge sensor technology and AI-driven analytics, the system provides actionable insights to reduce energy consumption, lower costs, and promote sustainability.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live data streaming from IoT sensors with 3-second updates

AI-Powered Solutions

Intelligent problem diagnosis with cost analysis and ROI

Smart Alerts

Automated notifications for high consumption

Savings Calculator

Calculate potential savings from optimization

Key Features

Real-Time Data: Monitor power consumption with live updates every 3 seconds.

Cost Analysis: View real-time costs with hourly, daily, monthly, and annual projections.

AI Problem Solver: Get intelligent solutions with implementation costs, ROI, and detailed breakdowns.

Automated Alerts: Receive instant notifications for critical/warning conditions with cost impact.

Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes with persistent preferences.

Savings Calculator: Calculate potential cost savings from energy optimization strategies.

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies:

Python Flask HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Chart.js

Use Cases

1. Campus Energy Management: Monitor and optimize across educational institutions.

2. Building Facilities: Track consumption in commercial buildings.

3. Sustainability Initiatives: Support green campus programs.

4. Cost Reduction: Identify high-consumption areas and implement solutions.

Project Goals

15-40% reduction in energy costs | Early detection of equipment malfunctions | Data-driven sustainability reporting | Improved operational efficiency
